Data Analysis Results

  • This is what very high positive correlation looks like. There is obviously a relationship between these two variables.
  • Somewhat dubious relationship in the sense that we are correlating a variable on a function of itself. We are looking at median home price compared to affordability index (median salary/median home price).
  • If salary increased proportionately with home prices, this would be a horizontal line. Since this is an upward sloping line, it means housing prices increase faster than salary.
  • Vertical lines represent states that are equally affordable but at different home price levels.
  • Horizontal lines represent states that have the same median home price, but different affordability index.
